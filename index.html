COMPANY OVERVIEW

Welcome to Panna Music Centre, Official Samsung Partners

Established in 1976, Panna Music Centre Ltd has risen to be the go-to technological partner for our customers and a trusted name in electronics and after-sales services. Located in the heart of Eldoret city, we are proud to be an authorized Samsung service centre offering genuine products and certified support.

Our commitment transcends transaction - We foster long-term relationships, provide future-ready solutions, and continuously adapt to the ever evolving world of consumer electronics and services. At Panna Music, your needs shape our innovations.
